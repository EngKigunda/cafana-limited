Who We Are

A Trusted Partner for Kenya's Public Sector

CAFANA Limited is a fully registered Kenyan company. We specialise in the reliable supply of goods and services to government agencies, hospitals, schools, and NGOs - backed by a vision to modernise public procurement through generative and agentic AI technology.

We understand the challenges of last-mile supply delivery across Kenya's diverse counties. Our team combines procurement expertise with technology innovation to ensure no public institution ever faces a stockout of essential supplies.

🔬 Research & Innovation Project

AI for Government Innovation

CAFANA Limited is developing SupplyMind Kenya - an agentic AI platform designed in partnership with county governments to eliminate procurement inefficiencies, prevent supply stockouts at hospitals, and ensure transparent, fair procurement for all suppliers.

This initiative aligns with national and county government development goals, targeting resilient economies and accessible health services.
